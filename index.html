About

The Origin: Our Library was born out of a passion for making great literature accessible to everyone, everywhere. We saw a gap between the convenience of big-box retailers and the soul of independent shops, and we decided to fill it.

How We Work: By combining a sophisticated logistics network with a deep love for the craft of writing, we ensure that the book you’ve been searching for is only a click away.

Values:

Sustainability: We use 100% recyclable packaging.

Support: A portion of every sale goes back to local literacy programs.

Selection: 50,000+ titles and counting.
